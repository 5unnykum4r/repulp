Executive Summary

Total revenue for Q4 2025 reached $4.2M, a 23% increase over Q3. SaaS subscriptions drove the majority of growth, while consulting revenue remained flat. Operating margins improved to 18.5% from 15.2%.

Outlook

Q1 2026 pipeline is strong at $6.1M, with 42% in late-stage deals. We expect to close the quarter between $4.5M and $5.0M if conversion rates hold at current levels. Key risks include delayed enterprise renewals and FX headwinds in EMEA.
