# Q4 2025 Revenue Report

Prepared by the Finance Team. Confidential.

## Executive Summary

Total revenue for Q4 2025 reached **$4.2M**, a 23% increase
over Q3. SaaS subscriptions drove the majority of growth, while consulting
revenue remained flat. Operating margins improved to 18.5% from 15.2%.

## Revenue Breakdown

| Category           | Q3 2025    | Q4 2025    | Change |
| ------------------ | ---------- | ---------- | ------ |
| SaaS Subscriptions | $2,100,000 | $2,800,000 | +33%   |
| Consulting         | $850,000   | $860,000   | +1%    |
| Licensing          | $350,000   | $420,000   | +20%   |
| Support Contracts  | $115,000   | $120,000   | +4%    |

## Regional Performance

North America continues to lead with 62% of total revenue. EMEA grew 31% QoQ,
driven by expansion in the DACH region.

* **North America:** $2,604,000 (62%)
* **EMEA:** $1,176,000 (28%)
* **APAC:** $420,000 (10%)

## Outlook

Q1 2026 pipeline is strong at $6.1M, with 42% in late-stage deals.
We expect to close the quarter between $4.5M and $5.0M if conversion
rates hold at current levels. Key risks include delayed enterprise
renewals and FX headwinds in EMEA.

### Strategic Priorities

1. Launch self-serve tier to reduce acquisition cost
2. Expand DACH sales team by 3 reps
3. Ship v2.0 platform with SSO and audit logging
4. Achieve SOC 2 Type II certification by end of Q2
